About us

Our Complex Litigation Group is staffed with an experienced team of knowledgeable whistleblower/qui tam attorneys and investigators. We are committed to helping whistleblowers recover taxpayers’ monies that were stolen via fraudulent activity against the Government.

Whistleblowers play an essential role in preserving the integrity of our nation’s Government funded programs. Thus, it’s our responsibility and commitment to ensure our clients have the necessary resources to properly pursue these types of cases, regardless of the size or complexity of the fraud scheme. Whether we’re jointly working in collaboration with the Government or litigating the case ourselves, our team is focused on protecting our client’s legal rights and winning.

We fight For the People, and we’re not afraid to advocate for justice in the face of the largest healthcare entities, insurance companies, top medical device makers, defense contractors, or any other fraudster who is attempting to defraud innocent taxpayers.

Our Experience to Your Benefit

Also, our whistleblower/qui tam team accepts referrals and co-counsel arrangements from other attorneys or law firms who know of someone with evidence of fraud. We want to create a network of allies who are willing to go the extra mile for potential whistleblowers, so we ensure that any co-counsel arrangement or referral is rewarded with a contingency fee according to the ethical and legal requirements of all relevant jurisdictions.

We take your case seriously. When you contact us for your initial case review, you’ll speak directly with a dedicated whistleblower/qui tam team member to ensure that your time isn’t wasted. Complete our contact form to receive a free, no-obligation case evaluation today to get started.
