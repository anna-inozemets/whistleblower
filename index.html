Know the Law

Whistleblowers protect taxpayer dollars and promote accountability in our nation's largest industries. If you are a healthcare worker, it is your duty to report Medicare and Medicaid fraud. As an American citizen, your right to report federal fraud in your workplace is protected by law.

Morgan & Morgan's Whistleblower Attorneys have the resources, experience, and compassion necessary to help you set right a wrong where you work. We are committed to ensuring that your bravery results in justice.

Our Process

The individual(s) who discover evidence of fraud are faced with a challenging decision, one that will have a notable impact on both their future and the future of many others. It’s a significant responsibility, but it’s more than just that — it’s an opportunity for you to do the right thing by exposing corrupt behavior and protecting taxpayer dollars. Morgan & Morgan’s Complex Litigation Group stands with these brave individuals, and when they’re ready to come forward to help preserve justice, we’re here to protect your legal rights.

Our Whistleblower Group is staffed with experienced attorneys and former FBI Special Agents, all of whom are committed to helping you reach the best possible resolution for your case. We understand your need for security and privacy during this stressful time, but you can be rest assured knowing that our team has done everything within their power to protect your confidentiality throughout the process.

Our experience is to your benefit. With decades of experience advocating for the rights of individuals seeking to expose fraudulent behavior, Morgan & Morgan is an undeniable asset in your pursuit of justice.
